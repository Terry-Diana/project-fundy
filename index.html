What is CrowdFunding?

Crowdfunding is the process of raising funds with the help of people across the country, using donation-based fundraising platforms as the medium. Crowdfunding on Fundhy helps for your startups.We support & expert assistance. Anyone can raise funds for their startups or for for their new project and ideas they come up with. With donation-based crowdfunding, getting financial help for startups is convenient and reliable. Unlike other loans and investments, fundraising via online crowdfunding platforms requires no liability to pay back the funds raised. So, all the amount generated can be used to cover the startup of company and grow if their ideas are geneuily unique.
